Delete Your Little One AI Account

You can request deletion of your Little One AI account and associated personal data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Little One AI app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account (if available).
  4. If you cannot access the app, email us requesting account deletion.

Email: support@littleoneai.com

Data that will be deleted

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Baby profile information
  • AI chat history
  • App preferences
  • Associated account information

Data that may be retained

Some billing records or information required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance may be retained for the period required by applicable law.

Processing time

Deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days after verification.
